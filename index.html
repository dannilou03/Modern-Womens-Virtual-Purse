Hot topics

Digital mortgage brokers

The new kids on the block habito provide a digital mortgage brokerage service online and via the phone comparing across approximately 100 mortgage lenders. Definitely one to check out to speed up the search for the right mortgage for you. Your journey begins by filling out details about yourself (as a one-off) and they will try and find the best mortgage given your circumstances. For residential, they have a much more sophisticated offering than buy-to-let (but that's the case for almost every provider).

Foreign currency

Revolut, Transferwise and Azimo (to name but a few) are quickly changing how we do foreign currency transactions. The Revolut money card offering is a must for travellers, debiting as close to the real exchange rate as possible wherever you go.

When will the app banks arrive?

App banks have long been in development, with plenty of marketing behind them and the banking licence well after. Monese is probably the one that's most prevalent in the UK, but people are put off by the monthly charge. Atom Bank and Mondo have been ramping up their marketing. Mondo release their waitlist, while Atom Bank remains suspiciously ambiguous on release. Meanwhile, check out robo advisors - they will text you critical information about your finances, aggregating info from all your accounts, answering questions like: "How much did I spent at Pret this month?". Alas, dear app banks, we're still waiting!
